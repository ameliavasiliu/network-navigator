Motivate progress

Prevent shallow task completion

Users may click future steps to preview, but not act

No task completion occurs in Roadmap tab

No checkboxes in Roadmap tab

Each roadmap step includes:

Objective

Why it matters

Preview of subtasks (read-only)

Additional Elements:

At top: Estimated Time to Hire

Based on role, geography, visa status, and task velocity

Display as estimate only (motivational)

At bottom: “Change in Circumstances” input

User can describe updates (visa change, interview, referral, delay)

This triggers roadmap adjustment logic

Only relevant future steps update (do NOT reset completed work)
